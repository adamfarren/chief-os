1. "Your FinStack investment was a financial data platform play. How do you think about the difference between data platforms and workflow platforms in fintech?" — Shows you've done homework, opens her thesis.
2. "Clarity Finance exited to a major ERP vendor. What did you learn about segment-focused ERP exit dynamics?" — Gets her talking about outcomes and how she thinks about our space.
3. "Your blog post argued fintech needs platforms, not apps. We agree. What would convince you we're building that platform?" — Directly engages her thesis.
4. "How do you typically engage with portfolio companies? Board involvement, operational support, customer intros?" — Practical and shows you're evaluating them, not just pitching.
5. "What's your timeline on Fund III deployment? Are you looking to lead rounds at our stage?" — Direct, appropriate for a first meeting.
6. "You invested in Beacon Analytics for the multi-entity buyer. We're seeing the same buyer for our enterprise tier. Would there be portfolio synergy?" — Strategic, shows you think about portfolio dynamics.
