## Purpose & Goals

Meridian Build Day is the launch event for the Agent SDK. It brings together developers who build on our platform to learn the SDK, build working agents in a single day, and become the first wave of our developer ecosystem.

**Goals:**
1. **Activate the Agent SDK developer ecosystem** — 50+ developers ship a working agent by end of day (tied to annual goal: Launch AI Agent Platform)
2. **Generate 10 publishable case studies** — agents built at the event become content for the developer blog and sales enablement
3. **Build community** — establish Meridian Ledger as the platform developers choose to build financial AI on (tied to positioning: "agent-powered ledger")

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Technical builders at mid-market finance teams and fintech companies — developers, engineers, and technical founders who integrate with or build on ERP platforms. They want to automate accounting workflows and are evaluating which platform gives them the best developer experience.

**Secondary:** CTOs and technical co-founders of fintech startups who are considering building on our platform vs. building their own infrastructure.
