# LinkedIn Post Draft

## The Post

Variance analysis takes 45 minutes per close in most mid-market finance teams. Forty-five minutes of a controller's day spent reconciling GL accounts, chasing down outliers, and re-entering data that already exists in the ledger.

One of our beta developers built an agent that does it in 3 minutes.

Not a demo. Not a prototype. Running in production at an e-commerce company with 200+ GL accounts.

Here's what happened:

1/ We shipped our Agent SDK last month. It lets developers build financial AI agents that run natively inside the close workflow. Full access to the ledger, the historical trends, the mapping rules.

2/ A developer at one of our design partners looked at their variance analysis backlog and said: "I can automate this." They built the agent in 2 weeks. It reads the general ledger, identifies material variances, pulls the relevant history, and drafts the commentary electronically.

3/ **The result: 45 minutes down to 3. That's 150 hours per week returned to finance staff at a single company.**

This is what a platform does that an application can't. We didn't build the variance agent. A developer who knows the workflow better than we ever could built it on our infrastructure.

The ERP industry spent 20 years building closed systems. We built an open one. The people closest to the problem are now building the solutions.

That's the unlock.
