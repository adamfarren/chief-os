## Overall Grade: A

The battle card is balanced and credible. The strengths section doesn't dismiss CoreLedger — it acknowledges real advantages (segment breadth, enterprise maturity) that sales reps will hear from prospects. The objection handling is practical, providing specific scripts rather than generic platitudes. The win/loss analysis includes losses and identifies the pattern (enterprise features, segment coverage), which directly informs where NOT to compete today.

## Issues Found

- **None material.**
- **Enhancement:** Could include a "When to walk away" section — deals where CoreLedger is the right choice for the prospect (e.g., 50+ finance team members needing 8+ segments today).
- **Enhancement:** The Recent Activity section could include implications ("their $30M extension suggests their AI strategy is expensive — expect them to raise prices").
